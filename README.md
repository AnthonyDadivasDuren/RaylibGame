# KILL CUBE

A fast-paced top-down shooter built with raylib and C++. Control a cube through waves of enemies, level up, and choose upgrades to become an unstoppable force. Survive as long as you can—and take down the boss on wave 10.

---

## How to Play

- **Survive** waves of enemies and collect **XP orbs** to level up.
- **Leveling up** lets you pick one of three random **upgrades** (press **1**, **2**, or **3**).
- Use your **pistol** and **shotgun**, **dash** through danger, and unlock **companions** and **passive abilities** to clear the screen.
- Reach **wave 10** to face the **boss**. Defeat it to keep going.

Your health is shown at the bottom right; when it hits zero, it’s game over. Your goal is to last as long as possible and rack up the highest score.

---

## Controls

| Action | Key |
|--------|-----|
| **Move** | **W** **A** **S** **D** |
| **Dash** (i-frames, damages enemies, grants shotgun ammo) | **Space** |
| **Switch weapon** (Pistol ↔ Shotgun) | **Q** |
| **Shoot shotgun** | **Left mouse button** |
| **Choose upgrade** (when offered) | **1** / **2** / **3** |


- **Pistol**: Fires automatically in your **current movement direction** (or last direction if standing still). No ammo; great for constant pressure.
- **Shotgun**: Fires on **left click**; uses ammo. Ammo is refilled by **dashing through enemies**. Shotgun kills can spawn **health orbs**.

---

## Features

- **Two weapons**: Pistol (auto, direction-based) and Shotgun (manual, high damage, uses ammo).
- **Dash**: Short burst of movement with invincibility, damages enemies and refills shotgun ammo. Starts with 2 charges; upgrade to get more.
- **Levels & upgrades**: Earn XP from kills, level up, and choose from 3 random upgrades (e.g. more health, damage, ricochet, pierce, companions, dash improvements).
- **Waves**: Enemies ramp up over time; every few seconds a new wave starts with tougher spawns.
- **Boss**: A special boss appears on **wave 10**; beat it to continue to higher waves.
- **Orbs**: Collect **XP** (blue) to level up, **health** (red) to heal, and **ammo** (for shotgun) from dashing through enemies.
- **Companions & passives**: Unlock pistol companions, orb companions, shotgun companions, auto-attacker, ricochet, pierce, death explosions, and more through upgrades.
